Hi, I'm <Nitya>

User Experience, Interaction & Visual Designer

"If you can't explain it simply, you don't understand it well enough"

- Albert Einstein

As a visual designer I enjoy the fluidity and simplicity of digital design and the thinking that goes beyond the pixels.

During my 8 years as an architect, building and remodeling spaces, I saw that I was only able to impact a small group of users with my work. Wanting to make a greater dent with my passion and skill for design, I turned to the web where I can build tools that potentially touch people all over the world

I want to use my seasoned design thinking process to provide simplified solutions based on heuristic analysis and a deep understanding of human interactions. I am excited about the prospect of working on websites and web-based apps for a caffeine-fueled company that values growth through learning and mentorship.

When I am not coding or sketching, you will find me exploring the best places to eat and going on adventures with my family.
