Hi, I'm <Nitya>

VISUAL DESIGNER & CREATIVE STRATEGIST

"If you can't explain it simply, you don't understand it well enough"

- Albert Einstein

As a visual designer I enjoy the fluidity and simplicity of digital design and the thinking that goes beyond the pixels.

As an architect, building and remodeling spaces, I saw that I was only able to impact a smaller group of users with my work. Wanting to make a greater dent with my passion and skill for design, I turned to the web and print world where I can create experiences that potentially touch people all over the world

I want to use my seasoned design thinking process to provide simplified solutions based on analysis, collaboration and a deep understanding of human interactions. I am excited about the prospect of working on design driven solutions for a caffeine-fueled company that values growth through learning and mentorship.

When I am not designing, you will find me exploring the best places to eat and going on adventures with my family.
